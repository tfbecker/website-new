A spaced repetition flashcard app with AI-powered card creation. The real innovation isn't the app itself - it's the workflow:

1. **AI-generated cards**: I can ask Claude to create flashcards for any topic, or pull from my Readwise highlights
2. **Feedback loop**: Each card has a comment section where I note "too superficial" or "needs context"
3. **Continuous improvement**: Claude Code reads all comments and automatically improves the cards

Built and deployed in 30 minutes with Claude Code. The entire app is maintainable through conversation.
